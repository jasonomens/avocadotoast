Avocado Toast Lore

  • Avocado toast has been eaten in San Francisco since at least 1885

  • In 1915, the California Avocado Association described small squares of avocado toast as hors d'oeuvres

  • Chef Bill Granger may have been the first person to put avocado toast on the menu in 1993

  • In 1999, Nigel Slater published a recipe for avocado "bruschetta" in The Guardian

  • Journalist and editor Lauren Oyler credits Cafe Gitane with bringing avocado toast to Instagram

  • A New York Times article showcased a "special" way to put serve avocado on toast in 1962

  • According to Lauren Oyler, avocado toast has surpassed grilled cheese as the go-to lunch in some demographics
